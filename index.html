About Nibi

A studio obsessed with the details that make products feel effortless.

We're a tight team of designers, engineers and strategists who believe great digital products come from craft, taste and measurable outcomes — not just deliverables.

Our story

Nibi started in 2014 as two friends taking on design work between projects. Twelve years later we're a multidisciplinary studio that's shipped over 120 products for startups and global brands alike.

We stayed deliberately small so senior people stay hands-on. When you work with Nibi, the people in the kickoff are the people doing the work.
